All about arrays.

array.push() - To add a new item at the last.

array.pop() - To remove the last item.

Array.length() is used to get the array length.

array.push() - To add a new item at the last to the specified array.

array.pop() - To remove the last item in the array..

array.shift() - To remove the first item in the given array.

array.unshift() - To add item at starting of given array.

array.sort() - To sort a given array.

array.concat() - To concat two arrays.

array.join() - To join two arrays.
